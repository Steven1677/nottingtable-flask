Hello!

Nottingtable is a web system that allows UNNC students to get their teaching timetable in ical (.ics) format.

All the data is crawled down from school's timetabling system, which is public on the Internet.

Please pay attention: it is not the iCalendar system released by the school's Academic Service Office!
